Copy this URL and paste it into your sideloader as a custom source.

How to Add This Source

  1. Open your sideloader app (Feather, AltStore, SideStore, or any AltStore-compatible IPA store).
  2. Go to the Sources tab (sometimes called Repos or Browse).
  3. Tap the + button to add a new source.
  4. Paste the source URL above into the input field.
  5. Confirm and wait for the source to load — 3lallah Repo will appear in your list.
  6. Browse the apps and tap any one to download and sideload it.
